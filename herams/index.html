Collectively build a comprehensive picture of the health sector in terms of health resources and services availability and monitor its evolution of the situation over time

The Health Resources and Services Availability Monitoring System (HeRAMS) is a collaborative approach that allows health sector actors systematically and continuously assess and monitor the availability of essential health resources and services as well as the major impediments to service delivery. It aims at providing the information necessary to identify needs, assess gaps and subsequently design and monitor the response.
