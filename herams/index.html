Collectively build a comprehensive picture of the health sector in terms of health resources and services availability and monitor its evolution over time.

The Health Resources and Services Availability Monitoring System (HeRAMS) is a collaborative approach designed to systematically assess and monitor the availability of essential health resources and services in highly constrained environments and provide decision-makers with the information necessary to identify needs, assess gaps and respond effectively.
